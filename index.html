Information Collection and Use

We collect only one piece of data: the Firebase ID associated with your device. This ID is used to identify your device for push notifications.

The app does use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

Link to the privacy policy of third-party service providers used by the app:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase

Data Transmission

Please note that the Firebase ID is transmitted over unencrypted traffic. This means that there is a potential risk of interception by third parties.

Data Storage

We store your Firebase ID in a Firebase database, which is a cloud-based platform provided by Google. This ID is retained for the duration of your use of the app, after which it is deleted in 90 days. Firebase employs robust security measures, including data encryption and secure access controls, to protect the data stored within it.
